Temple History

The Barranquilla Colombia Temple was announced on October 1, 2011 and dedicated on December 9, 2018. The dedication prayer can be found here.

When attending, plan accordingly by bringing your own temple clothing. Plan on eating nearby at a local restaurant, or bring your own meal to eat at a nearby park. This temple does not offer cafeteria services.

Common temple events here include sealings, weddings, receptions, and youth trips. No matter what will bring you here you will leave with joy and peace.
